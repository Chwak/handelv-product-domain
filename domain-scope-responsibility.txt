================================================================================
PRODUCT DOMAIN - SCOPE & RESPONSIBILITY
================================================================================

DOMAIN PURPOSE
================================================================================

The Product Domain manages all product-related functionality for makers, including:
- **Shelf feature**: Handmade products actively for sale - the maker's "shelf" of available items
- **Inventory feature**: Raw materials, suppliers, stock movements, and the "basement" (archived products)

The domain uses a single unified Product AppSync API to handle both features. Products can be moved between shelf (active sale) and basement (archived/unpublished). The shelf feature is responsible for the complete lifecycle of shelf items from creation to publishing, including media, materials, process steps, and certificates.

# Product Domain (Inventory feature)

## Purpose
The Product Domain (Inventory feature) manages all raw materials, stock levels, suppliers, material movements, and the "basement" (unpublished finished products) for makers. This domain is separate from Product Domain (Shelf feature) (which manages products actively for sale) and focuses on maker's workshop inventory management.

## Scope & Responsibilities

### Core Responsibilities:
1. **Material Inventory Management**
   - Track all raw materials makers have in stock
   - Monitor stock levels and reorder thresholds
   - Manage material locations within workshop
   - Track material costs and sourcing

2. **Basement Management (Finished Products Not On Shelf)**
   - Track finished products removed from shelf (unpublished/archived)
   - Store product metadata when moved from shelf to basement
   - Track basement product quantities
   - Enable re-publishing products from basement back to shelf

3. **Stock Movement Tracking**
   - Record all inventory changes (purchases, usage, waste)
   - Provide audit trail for materials
   - Link material usage to specific products/orders
   - Track material costs over time
   - Track product movements between shelf and basement

4. **Product Quantity Tracking**
   - Track total quantity per product when product is on shelf
   - Sync with Product Domain (Shelf feature) to maintain accurate counts
   - Update quantities when products move to/from basement

5. **Supplier Management**
   - Maintain supplier relationships
   - Track supplier reliability and lead times
   - Manage supplier contact information
   - Monitor supplier sustainability certifications

## Domain Boundaries

### What THIS Domain Handles:
✅ Raw material inventory (wood, leather, fabric, etc.)
✅ Stock levels and reorder thresholds
✅ Supplier information and relationships
✅ Material purchase records
✅ Material usage tracking
✅ Inventory movements and audit trail
✅ Basement (unpublished/archived finished products)
✅ Product quantity totals when products are on shelf
✅ Product movements between shelf and basement

### What OTHER Domains Handle:
❌ Active products for sale (on shelf) → **Product Domain (Shelf feature)**
❌ Product products and public catalog → **Product Domain (Shelf feature)**
❌ Order fulfillment → **Order Domain**
❌ Payment for materials → **Payment Domain**
❌ Product descriptions and media on shelf → **Product Domain (Shelf feature)**
❌ Notifications about low stock → **Notification Domain**
❌ Material supplier discovery/search → **Discovery Domain**

### Data Boundaries
This domain's data ownership is focused on raw materials and supplier records. Use these explicit rules to avoid duplication with Product/Order domains.

   - `basement_products` (finished products removed from shelf)
   - `product_quantities` (quantity tracking for products on shelf)

- Does NOT own:
   - Active products on shelf (Product Domain (Shelf feature))
   - Product descriptions, media, pricing for items on shelf (Product Domain (Shelf feature))
   - Order records or order item data (Order Domain)
   - Payment or refund records (Payment Domain)

- Integration Notes:
  - Consumes `OrderCompleted`, `ShelfItemCreated`, `ShelfItemUnpublished`, and `MaterialPurchasePaid` events to update inventory and movements.
  - Publishes `LowStockAlert`, `MaterialStockUpdated`, `ProductMovedToBasement`, `ProductMovedToShelf

- Integration Notes:
  - Consumes `OrderCompleted`, `ProductCreated`, and `MaterialPurchasePaid` events to update inventory and movements.
  - Publishes `LowStockAlert` and `MaterialStockUpdated` events for Notification and Discovery domains.

## DynamoDB Tables

### 1. material_inventory_table
- **PK**: `makerUserId`
- **SK**: `materialId`
- **GSI1**: By `materialType` + `currentStock`
- **GSI2**: By `stockStatus` + `lastRestockDate`

### 2. stock_movements_table
- **PK**: `makerUserId`
- **SK**: `movementTimestamp#materialId`
- **GSI1**: By `materialId` + `movementTimestamp`
- **GSI2**: By `movementType` + `movementTimestamp`
- **TTL**: Auto-delete after 2 years

### 3. suppliers_table
- **PK**: `makerUserId`
- **SK**: `supplierId`
- **GSI1**: By `country` + `city`
- **GSI2**: By `makerUserId` + `reliabilityScore`
